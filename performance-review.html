Overview

13 Weeks, Four Subsidiaries

Vanessa Chua joined Siddeley Group on 18 November 2025 as an ICT Business Analyst. On 26 March 2026, she was promoted to Head of IT, taking on Full Stack Engineer and Product Manager as primary duties, with Nicole, Eric, and Chloe as direct reports. Over 13 weeks, she delivered websites and digital systems for four Siddeley Group subsidiaries. She produced over 312 commits, wrote more than 41,500 lines of code, and delivered the equivalent of $80,000 to $180,000 AUD in external agency fees, in-house, at her current rate of $32 per hour.

Her approach centres on strategic insight: assessing what is missing, what is misaligned, and what will matter to each audience, then translating that into clear direction for her team or executing it directly. When leading a team, she works through structured delegation, task ownership, and regular communication. When working independently, she is thorough and autonomous, producing results to a high standard.
